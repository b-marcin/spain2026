🚗 Car rental — deductible & deposit
Lower deductible — zł 547.50pp –zł 169.50 · –24%

Deposit hold of ~1,030 PLN on your card. Less cash tied up. Lower financial exposure if something goes wrong.

Higher deductible — zł 717pp

Deposit hold of ~6,415 PLN on your card. Over zł 5,000 more blocked for the week. You pay first, then claim reimbursement.

Why the deposit matters even with full insurance: Even if you have comprehensive travel or car insurance that covers damage, rental companies charge the full deductible amount to your card at the moment of any incident — before any insurance claim is processed. With the higher deductible option, that means ~6,415 PLN blocked or charged upfront. You'd get it back eventually through a claim, but it can take weeks and requires paperwork. With the lower deductible, your maximum out-of-pocket exposure before reimbursement is only ~1,030 PLN. Unless the higher option comes with a meaningfully better or newer car, the lower deductible is almost always the smarter choice — less cash tied up, less stress if anything happens.
🌅 About Nerja
A beautiful Costa del Sol gem — known for the Balcón de Europa, Burriana Beach, the Nerja Caves, and whitewashed streets. September is ideal: warm 26–28°C, fewer crowds than peak summer, sea still perfect for swimming. Frigiliana nearby is one of Spain's most beautiful white villages.
